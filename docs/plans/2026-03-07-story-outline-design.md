# Story Outline Design

**Date:** 2026-03-07
**Status:** Approved

## Summary

Define the rough story outline for Pendulum of Despair — a JRPG homage to FF4, FF6, Chrono Trigger, and Secret of Mana. Original names, places, and characters throughout. The Pendulum is a MacGuffin; the true antagonist is Despair itself.

## Decisions Made

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| World setting | High fantasy overtaken by industrial magic-tech ("Arcanite Forging") | Blends FF4 medieval with FF6 magitek tension |
| Party size | 6-8 (medium ensemble) | Room for depth without spreading thin |
| Tragic romance flavor | Betrayal + separation + corruption | Layered tragedy across multiple acts |
| Protagonist structure | Two leads, one becomes betrayer | FF4-inspired brotherhood/fall dynamic |
| Villain motivation | Manipulation by abstract force | Tragic figure, not pure evil |
| Betrayer redemption | Sacrificial | Maximum emotional impact |
| True antagonist | The Pallor — Despair made manifest | Ties into game title, thematic depth |
| Factions | Three (Valdris, Carradan Compact, Thornmere Wilds) | Triangle of tension, neutral ground for exploration |
| Story structure | Four acts + interlude | "World of Ruin" midpoint shift |
| Ending | Triumphant but changed | Clear victory, old and new find balance |
