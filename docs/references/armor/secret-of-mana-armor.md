**Armor** is a category of equipment in _[Secret of Mana](/wiki/Secret_of_Mana "Secret of Mana")_. It encompasses protective gear for the head (hats and helms) and body (robes, dresses, and suits). Accessories (bracers, rings, and pendants) are also included. 
Each piece of armor may be specific to [Randi](/wiki/Randi "Randi"), [Primm](/wiki/Primm "Primm"), or [Popoi](/wiki/Popoi "Popoi"), shared by any two, or equippable by all. In 2D versions, the characters' portraits will appear when the armor is highlighted by the shop's ring menu cursor. In the 3D version, applicable characters will flash white. The rightmost column of the tables below attempts to follow a similar convention with respect to applicability. Accessories are applicable to all characters.

## About armor[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fmana.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FArmor_in_Secret_of_Mana%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]
Pieces of armor add extra protection from damage, and can be obtained or purchased all over the world. The prices vary, and sometimes items may only be obtained through specific measures. Some of the armor can be missable including Griffin Helm, Imp's Ring, Dragon Ring/Dragon Coil which the player must collect in Pureland, otherwise it can be missed..
