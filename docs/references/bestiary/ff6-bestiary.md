## Lists/Reference
### Bestiary Checklist
This section is not a listing of monster stats, but rather an easy way to make sure your end-game Bestiary is complete. Missable entries are listed in red; ones that can only be found on the Veldt or in the Soul Shrine at the end of the game, in purple. Remember that, in most cases, a monster must be defeated for it to appear in the in-game Bestiary; running away (or having the monster run away) is not enough, even though this is sufficient for it to appear on the Veldt, if that's possible for the formation encountered. **Best Location** is the easiest place to encounter the monster; note that the location may or may not available at the end of the game, so try to meet monsters as you go along.
