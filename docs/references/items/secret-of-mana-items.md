## Consumables[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fmana.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FItems_in_Secret_of_Mana%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]
Items used in battle for recovery and support. Consumables can be bought at shops and found in treasure chests dropped by certain [monsters](/wiki/Secret_of_Mana_Bestiary "Secret of Mana Bestiary") upon defeat. While each consumable item's maximum capacity is only 4 in the 2D versions (SNES/iOS), the player is given the choice to increase it to as much as 12 in the 3D version (PS4). The following tables detail the effect of and how to collect every consumable.
