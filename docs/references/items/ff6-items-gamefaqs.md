## Lists/Reference
### Items
This section lists out all of the items in Final Fantasy VI. The items are grouped by type (in the same order as in the menu). As such, most equipment is listed in order of power. Relics are divided into rough groupings and listed alphabetically within the groups.
Most of these tables should be self-explanatory. **Bet** indicates what item you win for betting the item and winning in the Colosseum. **Where Found** details where to obtain the item. However, most Morphs will not be included; Morphs will be included only if they are the only way to get unlimited copies of the item at the end of the game. Many locations for the most common items will also not be listed, for space reasons (typically, the easiest repeatable sources will be the ones listed). For shops, the item is always available anytime the shops are available at that location unless otherwise marked. Steals and Drops are always Rare unless marked with _(C)_. **Sell Value** is the price you can sell the item for; in most cases, shops sell the item for double its Sell Value. Items and locations in red are missable; in orange, limited in quantity at the end of the game but not missable; and purple, available in unlimited quantities at the end of the game but difficult to acquire. Entries in gray are beyond the point of no return.
Be aware that the versions of FFVI covered by this guide use a fairly random organization scheme within categories, especially for Relics. As such, _the order Items are listed below does not correspond exactly to the in-game default sort order._
For armors (including Shields), any Evasion or Magic Evasion bonuses are listed in parentheses next to the Defense or Magic Defense score, respectively.
