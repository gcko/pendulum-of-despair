List of the [weapons](/wiki/Weapon_\(equipment\) "Weapon \(equipment\)") in _[Final Fantasy VI](/wiki/Final_Fantasy_VI "Final Fantasy VI")_. Many [shops](/wiki/Shop "Shop") change their inventory over the course of the game, thus many of the equipment pieces listed on this page as being sold in a location will not be available for purchase at all times. 
Many of the weapons with [abilities](/wiki/Added_Ability "Added Ability") are affected by the [Mug glitch](/wiki/Capture_glitch "Capture glitch"). In the SNES version, all [Evade](/wiki/Evasion_\(Final_Fantasy_VI\) "Evasion \(Final Fantasy VI\)") bonuses have no effect due to the [Evade bug](/wiki/Evade_bug "Evade bug").

## Unused weapons[[](https://finalfantasy.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Fantasy_VI_weapons?veaction=edit&section=16 "Edit section: Unused weapons")]
[](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/5/56/FFVI_Unused_Sword_Sprite.png/revision/latest?cb=20250409175208)
An [unused](/wiki/Final_Fantasy_VI_dummied_content "Final Fantasy VI dummied content") sword sprite exists within the sprite data. It is likely intended for either the Flametongue or the Icebrand since those two use the same sword sprite. All three elemental swords (Flametongue, Icebrand, and Thunder Blade) have unique sword sprites not used by other weapons, Flametongue and Icebrand sharing a model in contrasting colors. 
[](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/0/04/FFVI_Unused_Weapon_Sprite.png/revision/latest?cb=20250409175239)
The second weapon is some sort of disc, circle, or orb. This is an odd sprite found in the sprite data. When made into a weapon, it behaves oddly. The character does not move forward when attacking, but simply stands there to raises their hands into the air. When attacking with it the user moves forward and raises their hands in the air, and a disc flies towards the target and disappears. After the attack, the character takes a step back from the original position they started. If the character attacks enough times, they will appear on the other side of the the battle screen.
