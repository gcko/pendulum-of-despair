The following is the list of [weapons](/wiki/Weapon_\(equipment\) "Weapon \(equipment\)") in the 2D versions of _[Final Fantasy IV](/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV "Final Fantasy IV")_ (except _[Easy Type](/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV_Easy_Type_weapons "Final Fantasy IV Easy Type weapons")_). Some weapons can cast magic spells by [using certain equipped weapons from the Item menu](/wiki/Item_magic_\(Final_Fantasy_IV\) "Item magic \(Final Fantasy IV\)") during [battle](/wiki/Battle_\(term\) "Battle \(term\)").   
The attack and accuracy camps show how much of these stats is added to the character's main stat. Some weapons may reduce the stats, while some may not add anything. An [unarmed](/wiki/Unarmed_\(weapon\) "Unarmed \(weapon\)") character will have a hit rate of 50% and the max hit rate is 99%. 
Some weapons are [glitched](/wiki/Equipment_bugs "Equipment bugs") and their [status](/wiki/Status_effect "Status effect") and [elemental](/wiki/Element_\(term\) "Element \(term\)") properties are considered as _armor_ in the original Japanese and North American _[Advance](/wiki/Finest_Fantasy_for_Advance "Finest Fantasy for Advance")_ versions; this bug is fixed in the European _Advance_ version. 
See also: [Final Fantasy IV Easy Type weapons](/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV_Easy_Type_weapons "Final Fantasy IV Easy Type weapons") or [Final Fantasy IV 3D weapons](/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV_3D_weapons "Final Fantasy IV 3D weapons")

## Bows[[](https://finalfantasy.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV_weapons?veaction=edit&section=6 "Edit section: Bows")]
[](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/e/e9/FFIV_SNES_Bow_Icon.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20130121013520) [](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/5/5a/FF4PSP_Item_Icon_Bow.png/revision/latest?cb=20120226043032)
Bows must be equipped along with arrows for them to work. They possess the [Throw](/wiki/Shoot_\(element\) "Shoot \(element\)") element. Bows with arrows are [long range](/wiki/Long_Range "Long Range"). 
Bows equipped without arrows increase Attack by 1. The attack formula with both equipped is (Bow attack power)/2 + Arrow Attack Power + Str/4. If the character equips a bow in his/her dominant hand, the total attack will be reduced by 20%. An unarmed character's attack power is Str/4 + Level/4, so there is the potential for a loss of attack power when equipping weaker bow and arrows.

## Arrows[[](https://finalfantasy.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV_weapons?veaction=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Arrows")]
[](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/2/2c/FFIV_SNES_Arrow_Icon.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20130121013520) [](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/7/78/FF4PSP_Item_Icon_Arrows.png/revision/latest?cb=20120226043032)
Arrows must be equipped along with bows for them to work. Arrows with bows are long range. 
Arrows equipped without a bow increase Attack by 1. The attack formula with both equipped is (Bow attack power)/2 + Arrow Attack Power + Str/4. An unarmed character's attack power is Str/4 + Level/4, so there is the potential for a loss of attack power when equipping weaker bow and arrows. 
There is no visual numerical counter that appears during battle, but the number of [hits](/wiki/Hit_\(term\) "Hit \(term\)") is used for the arrows upon hitting an enemy. The number of hits is related to the Attack Multiplier a character has. [Rosa](/wiki/Rosa_Farrell "Rosa Farrell") using the [Aim](/wiki/Aim_\(ability\) "Aim \(ability\)") command will ensure that the number of hits will be equal to her Attack Multiplier, regardless of the accuracy or hit rate of the current bow she has equipped. 
The number of hits is the number of arrows that appears on the target. It should be noted that the maximum Attack Multiplier a character can have is nineteen, so that is how many the player can see on the target.

## Harps[[](https://finalfantasy.fandom.com/wiki/Final_Fantasy_IV_weapons?veaction=edit&section=17 "Edit section: Harps")]
[](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/5/5a/FFIV_SNES_Harp_Icon.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20130121013617) [](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/finalfantasy/images/b/ba/FF4PSP_Item_Icon_Harp.png/revision/latest?cb=20120226043106)
Harps are long range and two-handed weapon weapons. A harp is required to be equipped on Edward to use [Bardsong](/wiki/Bardsong_\(Final_Fantasy_IV\) "Bardsong \(Final Fantasy IV\)") or [Chant](/wiki/Chant_\(Edward_ability\) "Chant \(Edward ability\)"). 
There is no visual numerical counter that appears during battle, but the number of [hits](/wiki/Hit_\(term\) "Hit \(term\)") is used for the music notes upon hitting an enemy. The number of hits is related to the Attack Multiplier a character has. 
The number of hits is the number of music notes that appears on the target. It should be noted that the maximum Attack Multiplier a character can have is nineteen, so that is how many the player can see on the target.
