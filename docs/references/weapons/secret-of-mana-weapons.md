**Weapons** in [Secret of Mana](/wiki/Secret_of_Mana "Secret of Mana") are available for all three characters ([Randi](/wiki/Randi "Randi"), [Primm](/wiki/Primm "Primm") or [Popoi](/wiki/Popoi "Popoi")) to use. There are eight different types. Once a weapon is received, it can be enhanced and/or upgraded by [Watts](/wiki/Watts "Watts") the blacksmith if the player has an energy orb for that type of weapon.   
Weapons information obtained at [Mana Knight's shrine](http://rpg.retropixel.net/mana/). 
## Contents
  * 1 Swords
  * 2 Spears
  * 3 Axes
  * 4 Whips
  * 5 Boomerangs
  * 6 Gloves
  * 7 Bows
  * 8 Javelins
