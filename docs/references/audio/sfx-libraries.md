# SFX Libraries & Tools

> Free and low-cost sound effect resources for Pendulum of Despair.
> Researched March 2026. Verify pricing and license terms before use.

## Free SFX Libraries

| Resource | License | Retro/JRPG Content | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------------------|-------|
| [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org) | CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-NC (varies per sound) | Search "8-bit", "16-bit", "retro game", "JRPG" | Large community library. Check license per sound. Free account required. |
| [OpenGameArt.org](https://opengameart.org) | CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-SA (varies) | Game-specific site with RPG SFX packs | Best free source for game audio. Filter by license. |
| [Kenney.nl](https://kenney.nl/assets) | CC0 (public domain) | RPG Audio, Interface Sounds packs | All CC0 — no attribution needed. Excellent quality. |
| [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/) | Free commercial use, no attribution | Limited retro content. General game SFX. | Clean, curated. Easy to browse. |
| [Pixabay Audio](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/) | Free commercial use, no attribution | Search "retro", "8-bit", "game" | Growing library. Can't resell sounds directly. |
| [Sonniss GDC Bundle](https://sonniss.com/gameaudiogdc) | Royalty-free commercial | Professional-grade. Not retro-specific. 20GB+ free annually. | Annual GDC giveaway. Check availability. |
| [ZapSplat](https://www.zapsplat.com) | Free with attribution; paid for attribution-free | General game SFX | Two tiers: free (credit required) and paid. |

## Retro SFX Generation Tools

These tools let you create SNES-era style sound effects procedurally.

| Tool | Cost | Platform | Best For |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| [jsfxr](https://sfxr.me) | Free (browser) | Any | Quick retro SFX — pickups, blips, hits, explosions. More NES than SNES but very useful. |
| [Bfxr](https://www.bfxr.net) | Free (browser) | Any | Expanded sfxr with more waveforms. Better for 16-bit sounds. |
| [ChipTone](https://sfbgames.itch.io/chiptone) | Free (browser) | Any | Most visual/intuitive generator. Excellent presets. |
| [LabChirp](https://labbed.net/software/labchirp/) | Free | Windows | Multi-layer sound design. More complex 16-bit era sounds. |
| [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org) | Free (open source) | All | Post-processing. Bitcrush/downsample any sound to retro character. |

## Recommended Approach for Pendulum of Despair

1. **Generate base UI/battle SFX** with jsfxr or ChipTone (cursor, confirm, cancel, hit, critical)
2. **Buy an itch.io JRPG SFX pack** ($5-$15) for a complete foundation
3. **Fill gaps** with Freesound.org and OpenGameArt.org
4. **Grab the Sonniss GDC bundle** if available — free professional SFX, process to sound retro
5. **Post-process** with Audacity: bitcrush, downsample to 22kHz/16-bit for SNES aesthetic

**License priority:** Prefer CC0 or royalty-free commercial. Avoid CC-NC for a commercial game. Document every source and license in credits.
