# Music Resources & Creation Tools

> Free and low-cost music resources for Pendulum of Despair.
> Researched March 2026. Verify pricing and license terms before use.

## Free Music Libraries

| Resource | License | SNES/JRPG Relevance | Notes |
|----------|---------|---------------------|-------|
| [OpenGameArt.org](https://opengameart.org) (music) | CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-SA (varies) | Search "JRPG", "RPG battle", "16-bit" | Best free game music source. Quality varies. |
| [Incompetech (Kevin MacLeod)](https://incompetech.com/music/) | CC-BY 3.0 (free) or ~$30/track (royalty-free) | Orchestral/cinematic. Not retro-specific. | Good for placeholder town/overworld themes. |

## SNES-Style Music Creation Tools

For creating original compositions in the SNES aesthetic.

### Trackers (Authentic Retro Sound)

| Tool | Cost | Platform | SNES Relevance |
|------|------|----------|----------------|
| [DefleMask](https://www.deflemask.com/) | Free (legacy) / $10+ | All | **Most relevant.** Has actual SPC700 (SNES chip) emulation. |
| [OpenMPT](https://openmpt.org/) | Free (open source) | Windows | .it/.xm/.s3m tracker. Use with SNES soundfonts. |
| [MilkyTracker](https://milkytracker.org/) | Free (open source) | All | .xm tracker. More retro-focused. |
| [FamiTracker](http://famitracker.com/) | Free | Windows | NES only (2A03 chip). 8-bit, not 16-bit. |

### DAWs & Browser Tools

| Tool | Cost | Platform | Notes |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| [LMMS](https://lmms.io/) | Free (open source) | All | Full DAW. Use with chiptune plugins + SNES soundfonts. |
| [BeepBox](https://www.beepbox.co/) | Free (browser) | Any | Quick melody prototyping. Simple, limited. |
| [JummBox](https://jummbus.bitbucket.io/) | Free (browser) | Any | Extended BeepBox. More instruments/effects. |

### SNES Soundfonts

Search for "SNES soundfont" or "SPC700 soundfont" at:
- [Musical Artifacts](https://musical-artifacts.com/)
- Community forums (SMW Central, ROM hacking sites)

These .sf2 files contain instruments ripped from actual SNES games.
Load into any DAW (LMMS, FL Studio, etc.) for authentic 16-bit sound.

## Recommended Strategy

1. **Immediate (development placeholder):** Buy an itch.io JRPG music
   pack ($10-$20) to unblock development with genre-appropriate tracks
2. **For authentic SNES sound:** DefleMask (SPC700 emulation) or
   OpenMPT with SNES soundfonts
3. **Free placeholders:** OpenGameArt.org and Modarchive
4. **Long-term:** Commission a composer who specializes in JRPG/chiptune
   (~$200-$1000 for a full indie soundtrack)
