## Key Concern: Asset Consistency

Mixing sprites from different creators will look inconsistent. Plan to
either:
- Use one artist/pack consistently for each category (characters from
  one source, enemies from another, tilesets from a third)
- Budget time for style-matching edits in Aseprite
- Commission a single artist for the final game

## Recommended Approach

1. **Prototyping:** Ninja Adventure (free, CC0) or Kenney packs to get
   gameplay running fast
2. **Production placeholder:** Buy 1-2 itch.io JRPG packs ($10-$30)
   for consistent style during development
3. **Final art:** Commission a pixel artist or do original work in
   Aseprite. A full indie JRPG sprite set (6 party members + 30-50
   enemies + tilesets) from a freelance pixel artist typically runs
   $1,000-$5,000 depending on scope and quality.
