# Palette-Swap Families

Each family is a base creature design with 2--4 variants at increasing
power levels. See [README.md](README.md) Section 7 for tier rules,
stat derivation, and naming conventions.

> **Status:** Act I families defined (Tier 1 only). Higher tiers will
> be populated in Sub-projects 2--4 as those act files are created.

## Wolf Family

**Base:** Wayward Wolf (Lv 6, Beast, Standard)
**Planned Tiers:** 4

| Tier | Name | Lv | Type | Element Shift | New Abilities | Threat |
|------|------|----|------|---------------|---------------|--------|
| 1 | Wayward Wolf | 6 | Beast | -- | Bite, Pack Howl (ATK up for all wolves in encounter) | Standard |
| 2 | Dire Wolf | 19 | Beast | Resist->Frost | +Lunge (back-row target), +Dire Howl (ATK + SPD up for pack) | Dangerous |
| 3 | Shadow Wolf | 33 | Beast | Weak->Ley, Resist->Void | +Shadow Fang (Void damage + Blind), +Pack Tactics (coordinated 2-wolf combo attack) | Dangerous |
